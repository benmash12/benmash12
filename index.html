ABOUT ME

I am a full stack web developer and graphics designer with a passion for creating custom Single Page Applications and APIs, among others. I started my journey in web development and graphic design in January 2016 during a holiday. I quickly became interested in creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites and applications, and started honing my skills in various technologies. Over time, I became proficient in using frameworks like React JS and Node JS, which I use to build high-performance single page applications. I also have experience in developing full-stack web applications using technologies like PHP, MySQL, and Java J2EE.

Aside from my technical skills, I am also a graphics designer. I use tools like Corel Draw and Adobe Photoshop to create beautiful designs (Logos and UIs) that complement my web development work, and others (cover arts and posters). With an eye for detail and a keen sense of aesthetics, I create designs that are not only functional but also visually appealing.

I am a hardworking and dedicated developer who is always willing to go the extra mile to deliver quality work. I take the time necessary to understand my clients' needs and work closely with them to ensure that their projects are completed to their satisfaction. With my skills and expertise, I have successfully completed numerous projects for clients across various industries, from Ecommerce to Social Media and Entertainment web applications.

If you're looking for a skilled full-stack web developer and graphics designer with a passion for creating custom single page applications, you just found one. With my expertise and dedication, I can help you turn your vision into a reality.
